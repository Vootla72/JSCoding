7. Axios Interceptors - Why
    Axios interceptors are functions that are called by Axios for every request or response. 
    They allow you to run code or modify the request or response before the request is sent or after the response is received. 
    Interceptors act as middleware, allowing you to modify the request or response configuration, headers, and data. 
    They offer a gateway to modify, authenticate, or handle errors at a centralized level.
